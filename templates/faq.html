Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a customer or farmer?

Go to the registration page and select your role. Fill in the required details and submit the form.

How do I place an order?

Browse products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You must be logged in as a customer.

How do I contact support?

Use the Help Center or Feedback page to reach out to our support team.

How do I become a seller?

Register as a farmer and complete your profile. You can then add products for sale.

How are payments handled?

Payments are processed securely through our integrated payment gateway at checkout.
